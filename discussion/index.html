About Me

John Michael Velasco

Self-proclaimed Reality-hater

Hello, for those who lost their way into my portfolio, bad luck for you, cause you will spend your precious time reading this nonsensical paragraph that I should say about me (but I wish I didn't because if you know too much about me, I'll be forced to put you on my watchlist -_-)

So, for starters. I enjoy movies, specially those with fantastical lores and backgrounds about them. I am a fan of Dungeons and Dragons, though I really don't know how to play it. Let's just say I am a fan when the topic or subject includes fantasy, myth, legends, or any supernatural lores. I am in love with the worlds between the books, pages and behind the screens. It is my way of escaping our reality and go into a world where I can be who I want myself to be.
